Olmo Giuseppe Certifications

ISO 9001:2015

The ISO 9001:2015 certification is an international standard ensuring that Olmo's products or services are manufactured or provided according to a rigorous quality management system. This demonstrates Olmo's commitment to customer satisfaction and continuous improvement of its processes.

OEKO-TEX® Standard 100 | 062694.0 | Appendix 6 - Class I

This certificate guarantees that the tested textile products do not contain any substances harmful to human health. Class I, the strictest, certifies that these products are safe even for infants and young children.

OEKO-TEX® Standard 100 | 23CX00123 | Appendix 6 - Class I

Similar to the previous certificate, this certification attests that Olmo's textiles meet the highest standards for safety and harmlessness, particularly suitable for sensitive use, such as for babies.

CERTIPUR | Application No.: C-IT-0720-126-0451

The CERTIPUR certificate guarantees that the foams used in Olmo's products comply with strict standards regarding health, safety, and the environment. The foams are tested for the absence of harmful substances and their compliance with durability standards.

HYGCEN | Identification No.: 99349.1 / 99398

The HYGCEN certification certifies that the products have undergone rigorous microbiological tests to ensure their hygiene and safety. This certificate demonstrates Olmo's commitment to providing safe, healthy products that comply with the strictest sanitary standards.

Toscana Gomma Certifications

CERTIPUR

The CERTIPUR certificate guarantees that the foams used in Toscana Gomma products comply with strict standards regarding health, safety, and the environment. The foams are tested for the absence of harmful substances and their compliance with durability standards.

OEKO-TEX® Standard 100 | Class I

This certificate guarantees that the tested textile products do not contain any substances harmful to human health. Class I, the strictest, certifies that these products are safe even for infants and young children.

IATF 16949

This international certification ensures that a company's quality management systems meet the strict requirements of the automotive industry. It ensures high-quality production, continuous improvement, and compliance with standards, essential for collaborating with global automakers.

ISO 9001:2015

The ISO 9001 certification issued by TÜV SÜD attests that the company applies a rigorous quality management system, ensuring optimization of internal processes and customer satisfaction. This certificate demonstrates the company's commitment to providing products and services that comply with the strictest international standards.

ISO 14001:2015

The ISO 14001 certification awarded by Certiquality certifies that the company implements an effective environmental management system aimed at reducing its ecological impact. This certificate reflects the company's commitment to sustainability, compliance with environmental regulations, and continuous improvement of its ecological performance.

ISO 45001:2018

The ISO 45001 certification issued by Certiquality attests that the company applies strict measures to ensure the health and safety of its employees. This certificate demonstrates the company's commitment to preventing occupational risks, reducing workplace accidents, and promoting a safe and healthy work environment.
