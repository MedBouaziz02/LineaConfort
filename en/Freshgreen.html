FRESHGREEN

FRESH WELL-BEING

Sensation of freshness

FreshGreen foams are produced with an additive designed to dissipate body heat, thus creating a sensation of great freshness and comfort.

FreshGreen is composed of particles made from a vegetable polyol, a raw material from a renewable and environmentally friendly source.

The cooling effect is not generated by a phase change, as in phase change materials (PCMs), but is intrinsic to the product itself.

Unlike other additives, FreshGreen does not affect the physico-mechanical properties of the foam and guarantees the quality of the finished product.
