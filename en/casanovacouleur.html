VISCO COLOUR

COLORS MAKE YOU UNIQUE

An added value: color

Building on the Casanova® experience, we've developed the new Visco Colour range, a low-resilience foam characterized by deep, vibrant colors that make each mattress unique and inimitable. Thanks to its advanced technology, Visco Colour foam meets the highest requirements while maintaining the essential characteristics of Casanova® foams: ergonomic, hygienic, breathable, hypoallergenic, anti-mite, and manufactured without CFCs.

Additionally, its chromatic properties offer extra benefits for sleep and well-being. Resting on Visco Colour foam guarantees a natural position, free from tension and compression, thanks to the thermal sensitivity of the viscoelastic foam that perfectly conforms to body shape and envelops it in a warm embrace.
