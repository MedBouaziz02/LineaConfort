VISCO PERFORMANCE

THE EVOLUTION OF VISCOELASTIC FOAM

Thanks to its low thermo-sensitivity (a result of the research and development of the Toscana Gomma laboratory), the technical characteristics of this foam remain unchanged regardless of the season (winter/summer). It guarantees stable comfort, adapting to different climatic and geographical conditions. This unique performance makes it an ideal material for the comfort sector (bedding and furniture), but also for more technical fields such as automotive, particularly in the development of new generations of vehicles for high-end European brands.

A FINE AND REGULAR CELLULAR STRUCTURE
A SOFT AND PLEASANT TOUCH

Upon contact, this foam provides an incomparable sensation of softness thanks to its velvety and silky surface.

Visco Performance foams have a weight absorption capacity thanks to their low elastic return properties. They delicately envelop the body and improve the sensation of comfort, even over a long period. They also offer excellent shock absorption performance in the automotive sector, optimizing not only driving comfort but also the safety of the driver and passengers.
