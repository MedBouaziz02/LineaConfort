OLMOSOFF

{/* Kept Product Name */}

A BREATH OF LIGHTNESS

{/* Translated */}

Padding has no limits

{/* Translated */}

Olmosoff is a new family of high-tech products with excellent physical and mechanical properties, offering numerous application possibilities in the field of furniture and bedding. Olmosoff foams have a density ranging from 13.0 to 45.0 kg/m³ and a low compression set, between 0.5 kPa and 2.0 kPa. These characteristics ensure a unique softness compared to foams currently on the market. To these important features, we can add extraordinary elasticity up to 500% and resilience greater than 40%. High-quality Olmosoff foams are also distinguished by excellent permeability and low permanent deformation.

{/* Translated */}

Space for new ideas

{/* Translated */}

The special characteristics of Olmosoff allow for new applications of polyurethane foam in furniture and bedding. Thanks to its high elasticity, low density, and excellent softness, Olmosoff enables the creation of high-value-added laminated and quilted products. Olmosoff is a valid alternative to polyester fleece and feathers, with improved features such as high air permeability and great elasticity, fundamental elements for healthy sleep.

{/* Translated */}

Exclusive performance

{/* Translated */}

Mechanical tests carried out at the German LGA laboratory have highlighted and confirmed that mattresses made from Olmosoff foams offer excellent performance combined with high air permeability.
